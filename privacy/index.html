Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Budgy is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal or financial data. Your information stays on your device and your private iCloud account.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information through the Budgy app. The app does not require account creation and does not transmit your financial data to us.

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into Budgy is stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. If iCloud Sync is enabled, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit.

3. iCloud

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is subject to Apple’s privacy policies. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. Analytics & Tracking

Budgy does not include analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, tracking pixels, or third-party monitoring tools.

5. In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information.

6. Contact & Feedback

If you contact us via email or our feedback form, we may receive your name and email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at hello@getbudgy.app.
